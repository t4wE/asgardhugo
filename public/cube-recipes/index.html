Cube recipes

First of all, remember that there are in-game accounts that contain the most popular items:

  • *items (has most hard-to-roll rares/crafts bases etc.)
  • *chars (has ready-to-go geared builds)

All items respawn every 2 hours so don't be shy to take anything you want from those accounts. You can as well simply copy chars from *chars account by dropping all they got on the ground and then picking it all up with your character.

If you couldn't find something to your satisfaction on those accounts - use the following FAQ and recipes to make items on your own.

FAQ:

How do I make Spirit in Monarch?

  1. Buy Monarch-looking Shield(Kite Shield) at Charsi.
  2. Upgrade it to Elite by adding 2 Thawing potions (=Monarch).
  3. Reroll it a couple of times (with nothing) until it's superior - 15ed/15ed (=Monarch 15/15).
  4. Add sockets by adding 3 TP Scrolls (= 15/15 Monarch 4 Sockets).
  5. Buy runes at any NPC and insert them.

How do I make Ethereal Exile in Sacred Targe?

  1. Buy ST Shield(Targe) at Akara.
  2. Upgrade it to Elite by adding 2 Thawing potions (=ST).
  3. Add Jah to make it Ethereal (= Ethereal ST).
  4. Reroll it a couple of times (with nothing) until it's superior - 15ed/15ed (=Ethereal ST 15/15).
  5. Add sockets by adding 3 TP Scrolls (= 15/15 Ethereal ST 4 Sockets).
  6. Buy runes at any NPC and insert them.

How do I make Arachnid Mesh?

  1. Buy Arach-looking Belt(Sash) at Charsi.
  2. Upgrade it to Elite by adding 2 Thawing potions (=Spiderweb Sash).
  3. Make it Unique by adding 1 Stamina Potion (=Arachnid Mesh).

Quick recipe: Sash+3 Stamina Potions (makes Elite Unique version of Sash)

How do I make Ethereal Sandstorm Treks?

  1. Buy SS-looking Boots(Heavy Boots) at Charsi.
  2. Upgrade it to Elite by adding 2 Thawing potions (=Scarabshell Boots).
  3. Make it Unique and Ethereal by adding 1 Stamina Potion and Jah rune at the same time (=eth SS).

Quick recipe: Heavy Boots + 3 Stamina potions + Jah (makes Elite Unique Ethereal version of Heavy Boots)

How do I make Aldur Boots?

  1. Buy Aldur-looking Boots(Light Plated Boots) at Charsi.
  2. Upgrade it to Exceptional by adding 1 Thawing potion (=Battle Boots).
  3. Make it Set by adding 1 Antidote (=Aldurs).

Quick recipe: Light Plated boots + 2 Antidote potions (makes Exceptional Set version of Light Plated Boots)

How do I make a craft?

  1. Just add a needed gem and keep it rolling.

How do I make a rare?

  1. Make it rare by adding 2 Keys (or 2 Keys+Jah to make it Ethereal and Rare).
  2. Upgrade it if needed (1 Thawing potion makes Exceptional, 2 Thawing potions makes Elite).
  3. Keep it rolling.

If you are sick and tired of rerolling items with needed affixes - you can install an auto-reroller. Watch the bottom of the"Mod info" section.

WW Claws

Firstly, upgrade Normal claw to Elite(+2 thaw potions).

Add 2 Keys to make it Rare

Add 2 Keys + Jah rune to make it Rare and Ethereal.

Reroll.

Trap Claws

Trap claws are Hand Scythe, Greater Claws, Greater Talons, Scissors Quhab. They spawn 15 strength, 5 replenish, and resistances instead of 300ed/450 ed, etc.

Make one of the claws listed above.

Add 2 Keys to make it Rare.

Add 2 Keys + Jah rune to make it Rare and Ethereal.

Reroll.
